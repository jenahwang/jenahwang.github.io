Research Engineer

[] [] [] []

I am a cognitive and a computational linguist in the field of Natural Language Processing. My research interests lie at the intersection of Linguistics, Cognitive Science and AI. I seek to understand how humans communicate and express meaning via language (and can be so creative in doing so!) and represent it such a way that can be used to equip machines to do the same. In more concrete terms, I am interested in the representation of meaning in context whether that falls at the level of lexical semantics (e.g., verbs, prepositions), constructional meaning or commonsense reasoning (e.g., social, physical).
