Research Interests

I am a cognitive and a computational linguist in the field of Natural Language Processing. My research interests lie at the intersection of Linguistics, Cognitive Science and AI. I seek to understand how humans communicate and express meaning via language (and can be so creative in doing so!) and represent it in ways that aid machines to reason about the world. More specifically, I am interested in the representation of meaning in context at various stages of expression, including lexical, constructional or commonsense.
